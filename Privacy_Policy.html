HealthEdPro : Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 18, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how HealthEdPro collects, uses, and protects your personal information. It applies to our website and any personal information obtained through communication with us. By accessing our website or interacting with HealthEdPro, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy at any time, and we encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed about how we use your information.

Information We Collect

This Privacy Policy outlines how HealthEdPro collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws.

  • Information you provide: This includes information you voluntarily provide when you register for an account, use our services, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to share.
  • Automatically collected information: When you interact with our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, referring website, pages visited, and time spent on our website.

Screening Tools

If you use our screening tools on the Site, you provide us with personal information regarding your mental health conditions, such as depression or anxiety. We also collect voluntary demographic information, including age range, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, state, and zip code. We endeavor to store screening data in a way that cannot be used to identify you. For example, IP addresses associated with a particular screening session are protected by one-way hash so that they cannot be traced back to the original IP address. We may provide deidentified or aggregated data to universities, health providers or others for research purposes, in all cases taking reasonable steps to ensure that such data cannot be associated with any particular individual.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services. We use your information to deliver the educational resources and services you request, such as processing course enrollments, delivering content, providing customer support, and sending important notifications.
  • To improve our services.We may use your information to analyze usage patterns, identify trends, personalize your experience, and improve our website and services.
  • To communicate with you. We may use your information to send you important updates, newsletters, marketing communications, and promotional offers related to our services. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time.
  • To comply with legal requirements. We may use your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Sharing Your Information

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us deliver our services, such as hosting providers, payment processors, and email marketing platforms. These providers are obligated to use your information only for the purpose of providing services to HealthEdPro.
  • Law enforcement: We may share your information with law enforcement agencies or other legal authorities as required by law or to investigate suspected fraud or illegal activity.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Rectify your personal information.If your personal information is inaccurate, you can request that it be corrected.
  • Request erasure of your personal information.In certain circumstances, you can request that your personal information be deleted.
  • Object to processing for marketing. You may object to the processing of your personal information for marketing purposes.
  • Data portability.In certain circumstances, you can request that your personal information be transferred to another organization.

Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect the personal information we collect. While we strive to ensure the security of our website and services, no online transaction is completely risk-free. It's important to follow good security practices, such as using a secure device and internet connection.All sensitive information provided to HealthEdPro is considered confidential, unless otherwise noted.We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. If we need to use your confidential information for any purpose, we will contact you and obtain your consent. You have the right to request that HealthEdPro remove your information from our records at any time. We will endeavor to process your request promptly, but there may be a delay in discontinuing related communications.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or by contacting you directly.
