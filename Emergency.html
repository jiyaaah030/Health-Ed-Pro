Emergency Guidelines

In case of a mental health crisis:

  • Seek immediate medical attention by calling the emergency helpline.
  • Stay calm and try to de-escalate the situation.
  • Provide support and reassurance to the person in distress.
  • Follow the guidance provided by mental health professionals.
